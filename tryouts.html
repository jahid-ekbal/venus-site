Requirements

Minimum Rank

Must be Gold rank or above to qualify for professional tryouts.

Age Requirement

Must be at least 16 years old. Players under 18 need parental consent.

Equipment

Must have stable internet connection (minimum 10Mbps) and current device for gameplay.

Commitment

Must commit to 15+ hours weekly training and tournament participation.

Rules

Fair Play

All participants must adhere to fair play standards. Any use of hacks, cheats, or exploits results in immediate disqualification.

Code of Conduct

Players must maintain professional behavior on stream and in team communications. Respect and sportsmanship are mandatory.

Availability

Must be available for scheduled tournaments and team practices. Excessive absences may result in roster changes.

Exclusive Participation

Professional players cannot represent multiple organizations simultaneously. Agreements are binding once signed.
